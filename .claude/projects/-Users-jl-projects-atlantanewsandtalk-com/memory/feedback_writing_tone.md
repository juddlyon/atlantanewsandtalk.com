---
name: Fun local blogger writing tone
description: Write like a local blogger at a coffee shop, not a corporate news bot
type: feedback
---

Writing should feel like a local blogger covering their neighborhood from a coffee shop — casual, warm, opinionated, with personality. Not robotic or corporate.

**Why:** The user wants the site to feel human and authentic, not like generated content. People can tell when writing is lifeless.

**How to apply:** When writing Claude prompts for summarization, instruct it to write with personality, use local references, have opinions about neighborhood stuff, be conversational. Think "your neighbor who reads all the local news and tells you about it over coffee."
